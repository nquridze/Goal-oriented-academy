My favourite foods

Pizza

Pizza is an Italian, specifically Neapolitan, dish typically consisting of a flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients, baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

xinkali

Xinkali is a Georgian dumpling filled with meat and spices, traditionally served with a rich broth inside. They are typically shaped like a pouch and are enjoyed by holding the top knot and taking a bite from the side to sip the broth before consuming the rest.

Mtsvadi

Mtsvadi is a traditional Georgian dish consisting of skewered and grilled meat, often marinated in a mixture of spices and herbs. It is typically served with fresh vegetables, bread, and sometimes a side of sauce.
